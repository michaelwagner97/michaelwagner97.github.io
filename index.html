My name is Michael Wagner, and I am working towards a professional writing major with a technical concentration at Michigan State University. This website contains previous works from college and my other educational and work experiences. I am pursuing a technical concentration because I would like to work in an automotive or auto-related field after graduation. Cars have been a passion of mine for a long time, and I would like to carry that passion into the workplace. Even my site's color scheme is auto-themed, since I have utilized the famous colors of legendary American racers and sports cars (such as the Shelby Cobra, Dodge Viper, and Ford Mustang).
